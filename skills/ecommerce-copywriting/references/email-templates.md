# Transactional Email Templates

Email copy for every post-purchase touchpoint.

## Email Copy Principles

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile preview)
- Front-load important info
- Include order number for reference
- Avoid spam triggers (ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation)

### Preheader Text
The preview text after the subject. Use it wisely:
- Extend the subject line
- Add urgency or context
- Don't repeat the subject

### Body Copy
- Lead with the most important information
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use headers to break up content
- Include clear CTAs
- Mobile-friendly formatting

## Order Confirmation

**Send:** Immediately after purchase

**Purpose:** Confirm order, set expectations, build excitement

### Subject Line Options
- "Order confirmed — thanks, [Name]! 🎉"
- "We got your order #12345"
- "You're all set! Order #12345 confirmed"
- "[Brand]: Your order is confirmed"

### Preheader
- "Estimated delivery: [Date]. Here's what you ordered."
- "Thanks for shopping with us — here are your order details."

### Template
```
Subject: Order confirmed — thanks, [Name]! 🎉
Preheader: Estimated delivery: [Date]. Here's what you ordered.

────────────────────────────────

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for your order! We're getting it ready now.

ORDER #[NUMBER]
Placed on [Date] at [Time]

────────────────────────────────

YOUR ITEMS

[Product Image]
[Product Name]
[Variant: Size M, Color Blue]
Qty: 1 × €49.00

[Product Image]
[Product Name]
Qty: 2 × €40.00

────────────────────────────────

ORDER SUMMARY

────────────────────────────────
Total:             €98.95

────────────────────────────────

[Full Name]

The [Brand] Team

## Shipping Confirmation

**Send:** When order ships

**Purpose:** Provide tracking, build anticipation

### Subject Line Options
- "Your order is on its way! 📦"
- "Shipped! Track your order #12345"
- "[Name], your package just left our warehouse"
- "It's happening — your order shipped"

### Preheader
- "Track your package → arriving [Day]"
- "Follow your order every step of the way."

### Template
```
Subject: Your order is on its way! 📦
Preheader: Track your package → arriving [Day]

────────────────────────────────

Hi [First Name],

Great news — your order just shipped!

[TRACK YOUR ORDER] (prominent button)

────────────────────────────────

TRACKING DETAILS

Carrier: [PostNL/DHL/etc.]
Tracking number: [Number]
Estimated arrival: [Day, Date]

────────────────────────────────

SHIPPING TO

[Full Name]
[Street Address]
[City, Postal Code]

────────────────────────────────

• [Product Name] × 1
• [Product Name] × 2

Contact [Carrier] directly: [Phone/Link]
Or reach us at support@brand.com

See you soon,
The [Brand] Team

## Review Request

**Purpose:** Collect social proof, get feedback

### Subject Line Options
- "How's your [Product Name]? ⭐"
- "[Name], got a minute to review your purchase?"
- "We'd love your feedback"
- "Share your thoughts, get [incentive]"

Subject: How's your [Product Name]? ⭐

────────────────────────────────

Hi [First Name],

You've had your [Product Name] for a couple weeks now. 
What do you think?

[Product Image]
[Product Name]

It only takes 2 minutes, and your review helps:

• Other shoppers make better decisions

## Email Timing Best Practices

| Email Type | Timing | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| Order confirmation | Immediate | Non-negotiable |
| Shipping confirmation | When shipped | Include tracking |
| Delivery confirmation | When delivered | If tracking data available |
| Review request | 7-14 days post-delivery | Give time to use product |
| Abandoned cart #1 | 1 hour | Gentle reminder |
| Abandoned cart #2 | 24 hours | Social proof |
| Abandoned cart #3 | 72 hours | Urgency/incentive |
| Back in stock | Immediately | High intent, act fast |
